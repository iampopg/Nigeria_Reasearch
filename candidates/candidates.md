# 🇳🇬 Nigeria 2027 Presidential Candidates — Master Research List

This document categorizes all known Nigerian presidential contenders for 2027 into:

- 🔵 CONFIRMED / ACTIVE DECLARED CANDIDATES
- 🟡 STRONG PROBABLE CANDIDATES (EXPECTED RUNNERS)
- 🟠 POLITICAL SPECULATIVE / COALITION FIGURES
- ⚪ MINOR / PARTY-LEVEL CANDIDATES

This is a **living intelligence file**, not a static list.

---

# 🧭 CONTEXT (IMPORTANT)

Nigeria’s 2027 election is shaped by:
- Coalition politics (multi-party alliances)
- Strong incumbency advantage (APC)
- Opposition fragmentation
- Ethno-regional voting dynamics
- Party switching and factional declarations

Key note:
> “Declared” does NOT always mean INEC-finalized candidacy — it often means public political announcement or party-level confirmation.

---

# 🔵 1. CONFIRMED / ACTIVE DECLARED CANDIDATES

## 1. Bola Ahmed Tinubu (APC)
- Current President of Nigeria (since 2023)
- Former Governor of Lagos State (1999–2007)
- Strong national party control (APC machinery)
- Incumbent advantage: access to state institutions, funding networks, security influence
- Status: **ACTIVE INCUMBENT & RE-ELECTION CONTENDER**

---

## 2. Seyi Makinde (PDP / Opposition Alliance)
- Governor of Oyo State
- Former Senatorial aspirant and long-time PDP figure
- Publicly declared 2027 presidential ambition
- Aligned with opposition coalition movements (PDP + APM-linked alliance discussions)
- Strong regional base (South-West Nigeria)
- Status: **DECLARED CONTENDER**

---

## 3. Peter Obi (Opposition Coalition Figure)
- Former Governor of Anambra State (2010–2014)
- 2023 presidential candidate (Labour Party)
- Strong youth-driven support base (“Obidient movement”)
- Currently aligned with opposition restructuring efforts (coalition politics)
- Status: **DECLARED / ACTIVE OPPOSITION LEADER**

---

## 4. Atiku Abubakar (PDP → ADC-aligned coalition discussions)
- Former Vice President (1999–2007)
- One of Nigeria’s longest-running presidential aspirants
- Strong northern political network
- Extensive business and political elite connections
- Status: **ACTIVE STRATEGIC CONTENDER (EXPECTED RUNNER)**

---

## 5. Rabiu Musa Kwankwaso (NNPP / Coalition-linked)
- Former Governor of Kano State (2 terms)
- Former Minister of Defence
- Leader of Kwankwasiyya political movement (strong youth following in North)
- Status: **ACTIVE STRATEGIC CONTENDER**

---

- Status: **HIGHLY LIKELY CANDIDATE**

## 7. Nasir Ahmad El-Rufai
- Former Governor of Kaduna State
- Known for economic reform policies and political restructuring ideology
- Strong elite political network influence
- Status: **LIKELY COALITION / PARTY ASPIRANT**

---

## 8. Goodluck Ebele Jonathan

- Highly respected international diplomatic figure
- Strong narrative of “stability leadership return”
- PDP faction reports suggest he has been cleared or endorsed in some internal processes
- Status: **POTENTIAL HIGH-IMPACT RETURN CANDIDATE**
