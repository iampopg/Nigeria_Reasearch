# 🇳🇬 Nigeria 2027 Election Research Hub

This repository contains structured research, data, and analysis on Nigeria’s 2027 presidential election.

The goal of this project is to compile **objective, evidence-based insights** on candidates, political conditions, and governance capacity in order to better understand who is most suitable for national leadership.

---

## 📌 What This Repo Covers

- Verified and potential presidential candidates for 2027
- Background and political history of each candidate
- Governance and performance-based analysis
- Institutional and economic constraints
- Political alliances and funding influence
- Policy feasibility and leadership assessment

---

## 🧠 Research Approach

All analysis in this repository is based on:

- Track record over rhetoric
- Data-driven political evaluation
- Institutional and economic context
- Independent verification from public sources
- Structured comparison models

---

## 📁 Repository Structure

- `/candidates` → Individual candidate profiles  
- `/framework` → Evaluation criteria and scoring system  
- `/analysis` → Comparative studies and reports  
- `/data` → Supporting datasets and references  

---

## ⚠️ Disclaimer

This project is strictly for **educational and research purposes**.  
It does not support or promote any political party or candidate.

---

## 🎯 Objective

To build a clear, structured understanding of Nigeria’s political landscape ahead of the 2027 elections using transparent and repeatable research methods.
