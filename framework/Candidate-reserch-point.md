# Nigeria 2027 Presidential Candidate Evaluation Framework

This document outlines key research domains and specific items to investigate for each candidate.

---

## 1. Background & Verified Record

### Personal & Professional History
- Full biography and age
- Education details (degrees, institutions, certifications)
- Career history (private, public service, national/international roles)
- Past achievements and recognitions
- Any criminal, corruption, or ethics investigations

### Public Service Track Record
- Offices held (dates & positions)
- Electoral history & outcomes
- Legislative, executive decisions & votes
- National reforms implemented

---

## 2. Performance-Based Evaluation

Measure evidence from real outcomes, not rhetoric.

### Records of Actual Results
- Economic performance (jobs, GDP, inflation, investment)
- Infrastructure delivery (roads, utilities, power)
- Security outcomes (insurgency, crime reduction)
- Education & health metrics
- Poverty reduction

### Crisis Leadership
- Performance during emergencies (security, economic downturn, health)
- Decision-making under pressure
- Transparency & accountability during crises

---

## 3. Institutional Context & Constraints

Investigate how political structures will affect leadership.

### Systemic Factors
- Legislature relationship (party control, antagonism, cooperation)
- Judiciary independence
- Federal vs state power dynamics
- Party cohesion and internal discipline
- Civil service capacity & bureaucracy

### Constraints on Implementation
- Constitutional limits
- Budgetary limits & revenue sources
- Security institutions alignment

---

## 4. Political Funding & Incentive Networks

Assess independence and external influence.

### Campaign Finance
- Major donors & corporate supporters
- Foreign links & international funding
- Influence of interest groups

### Power Brokers & Alliances
- Party elders & kingmakers
- Ethno-regional power networks
- Military/police influence
- Business conglomerates

---

## 5. Leadership Style & Psychology

Evaluate temperament and cognitive patterns.

### Decision-Making Traits
- Risk tolerance
- Logical vs emotional tendencies
- Receptivity to criticism
- Empathy & social perception

### Communication & Public Interaction
- Public speeches & debates
- Consistency of messaging
- Tone during conflict or setback

---

## 6. Policy Feasibility & Economic Modeling

Research concrete policy viability.

### Policy Specifics
- Economic plans (budget, taxes, spending priorities)
- Social welfare programs
- Security strategy
- Foreign policy direction

### Economic Forecasting
- Cost estimates & funding sources
- Impact on inflation, debt, exchange rate
- Job creation realism
- Stakeholder analysis

## 8. Sources & Validation

For every claim or fact, cross check with:
- Official government records
- Independent media investigations
- Academic or policy reports
- Public financial disclosures
- Court or oversight body findings
